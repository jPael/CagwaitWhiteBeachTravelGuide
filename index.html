Cagwait White Beach

Cagwait White Beach is a popular tourist destination in Surigao del Sur. It has fine white sands and a beautiful beach for families and friends to enjoy together. The beach is known as the "Boracay of Caraga," and the annual Kaliguan Festival is celebrated on June 24th. During the summer, it attracts many visitors.

How to get to Cagwait White Beach?

  • North
  • South

From North

Start your journey to the Tandag bus terminal, where you can grab a Bachelor bus to Butuan or Davao. Another way is to take a jeepney ride to Aras-asan, Cagwait for Php60 and an hour of travel time.
Tell the driver that you want to be dropped off at Cagwait intersection.
Take a habal-habal or tricycle to Cagwait beach for Php20 from the intersection.

From South

Take the bus bound to Tandag City and dropped off to Cagwait intersection.
From Hinatuan, take a bus to Barobo Terminal, where you can catch a bus to Tandag City.
